Life Trials-What we do
 
   When you join life trials,you get a personalised report,arming you with
            knowledge about the most important cutting-edge treatments that are
            showing promise in your cancer.
            
            You all find out how effective each treatment has been,how much benifit
            each one has given the people that have taken it,ass well as informing
            you about common side effects those people might have had.We also tell
            you where you can get each of those treatments,even if they aren't 
            widely available yet.
            
            The life trial services will be entirely free for everyone,and the reports
            will be curated by our world class medical advisory panel.Life trials
            doesn't sell any of these drugs,and we aren't associated with and medical
            companies. We just know how important these discoveries are to
            cancer patients, and we want to make sure you know about them.
